OUR CAPIBILITY

  • OUR CAPIBILITY

Parjet offers comprehensive seal manufacturing and distributing capabilities while believing in our 3 main values - Professional, Passional, Innovational. We strive to become one of the world’s leading sources for engineered sealing products. Our investment in developing sealing technology, manufacturing capability and research and development equipment contributes to our reliability, efficiency, and proficiency. Parjet can quickly identify, design, build and deliver all of your sealing needs. Our manufacturing abilities include seal design, billet molding, sintering, machining, skiving, cutting, and customized packaging.

Our facilities include:

  • CNC lathes
  • Compression molding press
  • Isostatic molding press
  • Sintering oven
  • Spring production machinery
  • Laser welding machines
  • Automatic seal-lip trimmers
  • Computer-aid seal friction and wear test equipment

Quality is our top priority. Parjet heavily invested in our laboratory with multiple research and development equipment including:

  • Universal Testing Machine
  • Particle Size Distribution
  • Differential Scanning Calorimeters
  • Thermogravimetric Analysis
  • Dynamic Mechanical Analyzer
  • Fourier-Transform Infrared Spectroscopy

These equipments allow us to analyze the different material compounds, maintain product quality, find the root cause of material defects, carry out incoming quality control and engineering consultancy. Additionally, with the Finite Element Analysis (FEA), we can simulate the behavior of a seal under given conditions. This technology saves time and money by projecting the outcome of a seal design without running the physical test. It is even used to identify and understand seal designs' pain points in case of failures. FEA has been widely applied for all of our seal designs to ensure that our customers get the best products. With this FEA analysis and our laboratory, Parjet will soon become one of the leading seal manufacturers in the world.
